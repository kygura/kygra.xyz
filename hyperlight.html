001 — Identity

A man out of time, witnessing the world as it was and as it is. Happily unemployed, refusing to trade my best years for a salary that supports a wasteful existence.

I sustain myself by the land and the markets, answering only to necessity. I work for myself and the betterment of others — finding freedom in having few costs and no master.

Technology is a
good servant but
a terrible
master.
